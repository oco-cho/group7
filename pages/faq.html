FAQ:

  1. What is your alcohol policies? You must be 21 years of old to attend this event. Bringing your own alcohol is prohibited. Purchased alcohols must be consumed in the event. Open bottles are not allowed to leave the event.
  2. What is your safety guidelines? We have zero disturbance tolerance. No guns or any other sorts of vapors are allowed to enter in the event.
  3. Is there a rain or shine policy? No, we offer indoor and outdoor booths so in case of rain you can enjoy our indoor area.
  4. Can I buy tickets at the door? Yes, but we recommend to buy your tickets in advance. Ticket availabilities will be based on first come first served.
  5. How can I request a refund? All sales are final unless San Diego Spirit cancels the even due to COVID-19 government guidelines
  6. Is this event handicap accessible? Yes, most indoor and our door areas are wheelchair accessible. There is limited handicap parking is available near the gate.
  7. How can I buy tickets? You can buy tickets online by clicking on Tickets or you can purchase them via third-party vendors or at the door.
  8. Where can I park my car?   9. Is there a student discount? We do not offer any student discounts, but we offer group discounts; if you purchase 10 or more tickets.
  10. I lost something What should I do next? All lost and found items will be kept at the main gate during the event. We highly recommend not to bring anything of high value with yourself. Valuables will be held for 30 days and then donated to a local charity. Fast Forward Events will not be held liable for any unclaimed items.
